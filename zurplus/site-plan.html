Overview

Purpose

Zurplus is a sustainable, nonprofit resource-sharing platform that connects businesses with leftover materials to those who can repurpose them, fostering a circular economy. Our mission is to promote recycling and repurposing across the United States, America, and eventually the globe.

Audience

We prioritize large manufacturing companies, followed by startups, SMEs, community groups, and local governments, providing them a platform to reduce waste and drive environmental change.

Dynamic elements

In Zurplus, JavaScript will be used to create an interactive experience with features like search, sorting, and filtering of listings by waste type. The code will be organized into multiple functions—for instance, an initFilters() function to set up event listeners, a filterListings() function that uses conditional branching (if/else) to decide which listings match the user's criteria, and a renderListings() function to update the DOM accordingly.

We will use ES Modules to structure our code into separate, maintainable components. Data representing listings will be organized as Objects within Arrays, allowing us to leverage array methods such as forEach, map, filter, and reduce for efficient data manipulation. DOM interaction will be achieved by selecting elements (e.g., filter inputs and display containers), modifying them in response to user events, and dynamically updating the view as users interact with sorting and filtering controls.

What is Zurplus?

Zurplus is a sustainable, nonprofit resource-sharing platform dedicated to transforming waste into valuable resources. Our platform connects businesses with leftover materials to those who can repurpose them, driving the circular economy forward by reducing waste and promoting innovative recycling practices.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower companies to view surplus materials as opportunities rather than waste. By fostering collaboration among large manufacturing companies, startups, SMEs, community groups, and local governments, Zurplus strives to build a greener, more sustainable future—one where every discarded resource is given a new purpose.

Vision

We envision a world where the circular economy is the standard, not the exception. Our goal is to revolutionize waste management on a global scale, starting in the United States, expanding throughout America, and eventually reaching every corner of the globe. We believe that by embracing innovative resource-sharing practices, we can create lasting environmental and economic benefits for generations to come.

About the Founder

My is Jose Velastegui, I am the founder and sole developer behind Zurplus, a dedicated software engineer with a passion for sustainability and innovation. As the only person responsible for building and maintaining this platform, I pour my expertise and commitment into every line of code to ensure that Zurplus not only supports a circular economy but also sets a benchmark for environmental stewardship in the digital age. Join us on this journey to transform waste into worth and be part of a community dedicated to creating a sustainable future.
